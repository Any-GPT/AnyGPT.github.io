AnyGPT:

Any-to-Any Multimodal Large Language Model

Abstract

We presents AnyGPT, the first autoregressive multimodal language model with the capability to comprehend and generate various modalities, including speech, text, images, and music. To unify multimodality, AnyGPT employs a discrete multimodal representation, harmonizing the representation space for both input and output. Utilizing the discrete representation,a new modality, akin to a foreign language, can be seamlessly integrated without necessitating alterations to the existing LLM architecture or training objectives. We construct an extensive corpus of text-centric multimodal alignment data for pretrain, In order to adapt the model for handling arbitrary combinations of multimodal input and output, we utilize advanced generative models, synthesizing a large-scale instruction dataset named MMIChat, comprising 108k samples of multi-turn conversations that feature a complex interweaving of multimodal elements. % Experimental results demonstrate that AnyGPT achieves comparable performance to specialized models in each modality, while also exhibiting an impressive capacity for Any-to-Any multimodal dialogue. This showcases a potential form of AGI for future applications.
